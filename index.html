A little info about myself...

My name is Latifat Adisa, I'm currently enrolled as a Cloud engineering student at AltSchool Africa and my student ID is: ALT/SOE/025/3663. I am a very curious and driven individual with a growing passion for technology and its potential to solve real-world problems.

I come from a non-technical background, but I have always been fascinated by how digital systems work, especially in Cloud computing.

So why are we here?...

Making the big switch into tech has been a journey filled with curiosity, resilience, and a fair share of trial and error. As a self-taught learner with no prior understanding of the tech ecosysytem, I have had to explore multiple fields ranging from Backend and Frontend development to dabbling into Cybersecurity courses on Udemy.

Through this process, I discovered Cloud computing, both as a concept and a career path. The concept of reimagined access, the idea of being able to scale, deploy, and manage systems remotely resonated deeply with me. But I quickly realized that passion alone wasn't enough;I needed structure, community,and a clearly defined path.

I joined this program at AltSchool beacuse it offers the structured learning environment I have been seeking, one that would help me commit fully to my career goals and also equip me with the skills needed to thrive in the tech ecosystem.

What exactly are my goals for the S.O.E?...

My goal is to become a well-rounded cloud engineer who can contribute to building scalable, secure, and efficient systems. I want to master cloud infrastructures, improve my problem-solving skills, and gain real-world experience through hands-on projects.

Beyond technical mastery, I'm deeply committed to contributing to the future of Altschool and the School of Enginnering. I want to be part of its robust alumni network, not just as a graduate but as a bridge. I hope to foster growth in the community and also create opportunities for others.

In the long term, I'd love to create scholarship programs, mentor future students, and advocate for access to quality tech education across communities. By actively giving back, I want my journey to inspire and empower others to become everything they've hoped to be.
